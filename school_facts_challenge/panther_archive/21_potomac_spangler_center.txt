THE SPANGLER CENTER

The Spangler Center for Athletics and Community opened at The Potomac School in 2019. It was designed for more than competitive games. The building includes a competition gym, practice space, an indoor walking and jogging track, a fitness center, strength and conditioning areas, meeting and event rooms, and a multipurpose studio.

The gym can be arranged as two full-size competition courts or divided to create three practice courts. The center also includes seven competition squash courts. Nearby tennis facilities contain eight courts.

Its full name is important: Spangler Center for Athletics and Community. That wording reflects the building's use for exercise, competition, meetings, and school gatherings. It is one of many facilities on Potomac's 90-acre McLean campus.
