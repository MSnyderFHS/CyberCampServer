MASCOT FIELD NOTES

Flint Hill's mascot is the Husky. The school previously used the Falcons name, but students selected the Husky in a vote during the early 1990s. Flint Hill connects the mascot to cooperation: members of a sled team must pull together to reach the finish line. The school's costumed Husky is known as Klondike.

The Potomac School's athletic identity is the Panthers. School publications, team pages, and championship records all use the Panther name. The Panther appears at community and athletic events and represents the green-and-orange school community.

A husky and a panther would rarely meet in the wild. In Northern Virginia school athletics, however, the mascots can meet whenever Flint Hill and Potomac teams compete. That makes them a natural pair for a friendly rivalry story.
