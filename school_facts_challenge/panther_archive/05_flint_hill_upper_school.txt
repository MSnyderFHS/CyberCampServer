NOTES ON FLINT HILL'S UPPER SCHOOL

Flint Hill opened its current Upper School for the 2001-02 school year. The building included spaces for science, visual arts, athletics, languages, mathematics, and a Learning Center. The school says that the Learning Center was among the first programs of its kind in the region to provide direct support for students with different learning needs.

The Upper School serves grades 9-12. Flint Hill describes the program as rigorous but supportive, with increasing freedom and choice intended to prepare students for college. Its website highlights hands-on learning, senior projects, real-world connections, and student-designed clubs.

The Upper School campus is located at 3320 Jermantown Road in Oakton, Virginia.
