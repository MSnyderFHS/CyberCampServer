FLINT HILL ACTIVITY CREDITS

Flint Hill Upper School students must earn four activity credits before graduation. The requirement is intended to encourage physical activity, teamwork, and involvement beyond regular classroom courses.

Athletic teams are one way to earn credits, but Flint Hill also recognizes selected group activities. Its Upper School information names such options as plays and musicals, vocal music, Certamen, yearbook, literary magazine, the student newspaper, robotics teams, and the after-school cybersecurity activity.

This structure treats clubs, publications, performing groups, competitive teams, and technical programs as places where students can practice commitment and cooperation. It also lets students with different interests contribute to the school community while working toward the same graduation requirement.
