THE ENGELHARD PERFORMING ARTS CENTER

The Potomac School's campus includes the Engelhard Performing Arts Center. According to the school, the center has music rehearsal rooms, an art gallery, and an auditorium with 576 seats. These spaces support performances as well as everyday instruction and rehearsal.

Potomac's broader campus also includes art and photography studios, libraries, science and computer labs, and many common areas. The combination reflects the school's K-12 structure: students of different ages can learn, create, perform, and attend events on the same wooded campus.

The performing arts center is one part of a campus that mixes indoor and outdoor learning. Not far away are athletic spaces, classroom buildings, wooded trails, ponds, gardens, and outdoor classrooms.
