FLINT HILL BY THE NUMBERS

Flint Hill describes itself as an independent JK-12 school in Oakton, Virginia. For the 2025-26 school year, its history page reports 1,062 students. The main school website describes 50 acres across two campuses.

The school organizes its academic program into Lower School, Middle School, and Upper School divisions. The Upper School covers grades 9-12. Flint Hill's website also lists eight college-preparatory academic departments, dozens of fine arts courses, and a large collection of athletic teams.

Numbers can change from one school year to the next, but the overall picture is consistent: Flint Hill is a multi-division school with academic, artistic, athletic, and extracurricular programs. Its facilities are located at several Oakton addresses rather than on one single continuous site.
