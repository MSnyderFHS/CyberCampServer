FLINT HILL GROWTH TIMELINE

1956: Flint Hill Preparatory was founded in Fairfax.

1989: Til Hazel led an acquisition and reorganization of the school.

1990: Flint Hill School opened on the site now called Hazel Lower School, serving kindergarten through grade 12.

Early 1990s: Students voted to replace the Falcon mascot with the Husky.

2001-02: The current Upper School opened with new academic, arts, athletic, and student-support facilities.

2005: Flint Hill celebrated its 50th anniversary.

2011-12: The school introduced a one-to-one technology program and received recognition as an Apple Distinguished School.

2020-21: Students in grades 7 and 8 moved into the new Peterson Middle School.

The milestones reflect repeated changes in name, facilities, technology, and student life.

Source: https://www.flinthill.org/our-history/
