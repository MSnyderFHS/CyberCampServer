FLINT HILL ATHLETICS SNAPSHOT

Flint Hill offers interscholastic sports in fall, winter, and spring. The school's current athletics pages list 22 sports and 89 teams across the Middle and Upper Schools. Offerings include cross-country, field hockey, football, soccer, tennis, volleyball, basketball, climbing, ice hockey, swimming, diving, baseball, lacrosse, softball, and track and field.

Middle School athletics take place within the school day. Upper School practices normally begin after academic classes. Flint Hill expects Upper School students to complete activity credits, and athletics are one of the major ways students can earn them.

The school describes athletics as part of the educational program, emphasizing teamwork, discipline, sportsmanship, commitment, and personal growth in addition to competition.
