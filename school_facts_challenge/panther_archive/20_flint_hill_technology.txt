FLINT HILL AND ONE-TO-ONE TECHNOLOGY

Flint Hill introduced a one-to-one technology program during the 2011-12 school year. The school was named an Apple Distinguished School, a designation associated with educational innovation, leadership, and the use of technology in learning.

Flint Hill's current program provides iPads to Lower School students and MacBook Air computers to Middle and Upper School students. A common device platform can make it easier for teachers to plan digital projects, collaboration, research, and creative work.

Technology is only one part of the school's program. Flint Hill also emphasizes hands-on learning, arts, athletics, student clubs, senior projects, and real-world connections. The presence of devices across grade levels means digital tools can support many subjects instead of belonging only to computer classes.
