POTOMAC ATHLETIC FACILITIES

The Potomac School uses its large McLean campus for a wide range of sports. Its outdoor facilities include a turf field used for football, lacrosse, soccer, and field hockey; three grass fields; baseball and softball fields; a six-lane track; and a cross-country course through the campus.

Indoor facilities include the Spangler Center for Athletics and Community. The center contains competition and practice courts, an indoor walking and jogging track, fitness and conditioning areas, meeting rooms, and a multipurpose studio. Potomac also lists squash courts in the Spangler Center and Chester Gym, along with an eight-court tennis facility nearby.

Potomac teams use the Panther name. The variety of facilities allows many sports to operate during the same season.
