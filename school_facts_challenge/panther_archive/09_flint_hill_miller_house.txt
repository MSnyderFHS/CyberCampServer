THE MILLER HOUSE

Flint Hill has operated on four campuses during its history, but Miller House remains a visible connection to the past. It stands at 10409 Academic Drive in Oakton and is now used as office space.

Parts of Miller House came from the Willcoxon Inn, a building constructed in 1805. This makes the structure much older than Flint Hill School, which dates its founding to 1956. The contrast is striking: a modern JK-12 school uses a building with architectural elements created more than a century before the school existed.

The Academic Drive site is now associated with Hazel Lower School. Flint Hill's Middle School and Upper School use other locations in Oakton, so the school operates across multiple nearby sites rather than on one continuous campus.
